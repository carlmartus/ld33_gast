The objective of the game is to sneak passed all the people standing around. You are a monster that naturally lurks in the dark and has the ability to see in it. Do not let anyone see you in the light. If anyone sees you, you will get busted and the level will restart.

Keys:
UP - move up
DOWN -  move down
LEFT - move left
RIGHT move right

This game is turned out to be more of tech demo than a game but I figured I'd post it anyway since it's easy to play. Just press the link to play the game.
